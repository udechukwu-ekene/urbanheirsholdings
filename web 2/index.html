Introduction

Urban Heirs Holdings is a dynamic and diversified group of companies operating in various sectors including Real Estate, Property Management, Logistics and Procurement, Construction & Civil Services, General Contracting, Account Auditing and Legal Services. With a strong commitment to excellence and innovation. Urban Heirs Holdings has established itself as a leader in the industry.

In the Real Estate sector, Urban Heirs Holdings has a range of subsidiaries offering services such as short-term and hotel accommodations, warehouse and event center leasing, land sales and leasing, as well as house letting and purchase. Their expert team ensures that clients receive top-notch service and guidance throughout their real estate transactions.

The Property Management arm of Urban Heirs Holdings specializes in housing and company management, industrial cleaning and janitor services, post-construction cleanup, waste management, environmental management planning, environmental and social impact assessment (ESIA), and environmental audits. They are dedicated to maintaining properties to the highest standards while also promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Urban Heirs Holdings also excels in Logistics and Procurement services, providing efficient solutions for supply chain management and procurement needs. Their expertise in this area ensures seamless operations for businesses of all sizes.

In the Construction & Civil Services sector, Urban Heirs Holdings offers a wide range of services including construction management, civil engineering, and project development. Their team of professionals is known for delivering projects on time and within budget.

As General Contractors, Urban Heirs Holdings has a proven track record of successfully completing projects across various industries. Their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets them apart in the competitive contracting market.

Lastly, Urban Heirs Holdings offers expert Account Auditing and Legal Services to ensure compliance with financial regulations and legal requirements. Their team of experienced auditors and legal professionals provides comprehensive support to businesses seeking to maintain transparency and integrity in their operations.

With a focus on excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Urban Heirs Holdings continues to set new standards in the industry and remains a trusted partner for clients seeking reliable and professional services across multiple sectors.
